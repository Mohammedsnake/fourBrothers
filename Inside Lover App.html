Short History:**Our Love Story** We met at the Laureate International School (LIS) in Zanzibar. MO fell in love first. Both MO and Zuhra were studying at LIS, but MO joined in the 9th grade, while Zuhra had been there since baby class.

It was in the 9th grade that MO first met Zuhra. They were in the same class, studying the same subjects. MO's feelings began to blossom after seeing Zuhra. Zuhra was a quiet girl who hardly spoke to any boys in the class. She always kept to herself, and this was an obstacle for MO to approach her.

So, he decided to hide his feelings until the right time came. In the 10th grade, the boys in their class started to build friendships with Zuhra, engaging in group discussions and working on projects together. This made MO a bit jealous, and he decided it was time to express his feelings.

He planned to tell Zuhra the truth about his love for her. MO set aside some free time and found Zuhra in her classroom. He poured out his feelings to her one evening on campus. It was a significant moment, but MO couldn't keep his emotions bottled up any longer. Zuhra, being the shy and quiet person she was, couldn't accept his feelings immediately. She had never received such a confession before and was both surprised and shocked, leading her to refuse MO's proposal.

Despite the refusal, MO continued with his studies and decided to give Zuhra some space. Days passed, and they resumed being normal classmates. After the 10th grade, something changed. Zuhra approached MO and shared her own feelings. She revealed that she had been in love with him for some time but was just too afraid to admit it. Since then, our journey has been filled with love and joy.
